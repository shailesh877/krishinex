Who We Are

Revolutionizing Indian Agriculture

KrishiNex, a flagship brand under Knexis Technologies Pvt. Ltd., is India’s most innovative Agri-Fintech platform. We are building a comprehensive, technology-driven ecosystem designed to bridge the massive gap between traditional farming practices and modern financial and agricultural technology.

Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian economy, yet the farmers who sustain the nation often struggle with fragmented supply chains, lack of real-time agricultural advisory, and, most critically, limited access to instant financial liquidity. KrishiNex was established to solve these exact challenges from the grassroots level up.

By integrating 'Smart Farming' with 'Smart Finance,' we are creating a seamless network where farmers are not just consumers, but empowered entrepreneurs.

Our Core Ecosystem

A Fully Integrated Lifecycle Solution

We do not just sell products; we deliver a fully integrated agricultural lifecycle solution.

The NexCard

A revolutionary, first-of-its-kind smart membership and financial card that grants farmers 24x7 access to cash withdrawals, easy agri-finance, and direct subsidy benefits.

KrishiNex Sewa Points (KSP)

Our asset-light, highly scalable franchise network that upgrades existing rural agri-shops into modern, tech-enabled fulfillment centers. KSPs serve as the physical touchpoint for all KrishiNex services, ensuring trust and accessibility.

Agriculture Drone (ADaaS)

Bringing precision agriculture to the village level. We provide advanced drone spraying and crop monitoring services, saving farmers time, reducing chemical exposure, and maximizing crop yields.

Premium Agri-Inputs

Direct access to high-quality, authentic seeds, fertilizers, and tools at fair market prices, eliminating middlemen and ensuring crop security.

Our Vision

To build India's most trusted, transparent, and farmer-centric Agri-Fintech ecosystem that financially empowers every farmer, modernizes rural agriculture, and ensures absolute food and financial security for the nation.

Our Mission

To bring 1 million farmers into the formal digital and financial economy within the next five years through the NexCard, while scaling our KSP network to every major agricultural block in the country.

Founder's Message

To our Farmers, Partners, and Investors,

When I look at the Indian agricultural landscape, I don't just see fields; I see the untapped potential of millions of rural entrepreneurs. For generations, the Indian farmer has fed this country while fighting a daily battle against unpredictable weather, broken supply chains, and a systemic lack of financial support.

I founded KrishiNex because I realized that offering a farmer better seeds or fertilizers is only solving half the problem. The real crisis is liquidity. When a farmer needs cash for an emergency at 2:00 AM, or when they need immediate capital to save a failing crop, traditional banking systems are often too slow or completely inaccessible. That is the exact moment a farmer is forced into the hands of local moneylenders.

That is why we created the NexCard. It is more than just a piece of plastic; it is a symbol of financial independence and dignity. By pairing this with our network of KrishiNex Sewa Points (KSP), we are bringing the bank, the advisory center, and the modern agri-store directly to the farmer's village. Furthermore, by introducing cutting-edge technologies like Agriculture Drone Services, we are ensuring that the farmer of today is equipped with the tools of tomorrow.

Our approach at Knexis Technologies is completely asset-light and highly scalable, but our impact is deeply rooted in the soil. We are creating a self-sustaining ecosystem where everyone wins—the farmer gets liquidity and tech, the local KSP partner grows their business, and the agricultural supply chain becomes transparent and efficient.

We are on a mission to redefine what it means to be a farmer in India. We are restoring 'Kisan Gaurav'—the pride of the farmer. I invite you to join us on this incredible journey as we connect the fields and scale the future.

Warm Regards,

Kishan Pandit
